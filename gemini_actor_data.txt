**Body Type:**
Hourglass silhouette with a well-defined waist, proportionate shoulders and hips. This body type exudes femininity and can carry a variety of clothing styles.

**Color Palette:**
Warm skin tones with golden undertones allow for a diverse palette, including:
* Warm Neutrals: Cream, beige, soft browns
* Jewel Tones: Deep red, emerald green, royal blue
* Rich, Earthy Tones: Mustard yellow, burnt orange, olive green
